Who We Are

Welcome to Pick the Game—the easiest way to manage your football pools and make the season more fun! If you’ve ever found yourself buried in spreadsheets, chasing down picks, and just trying to keep everything organized, we’re here to change that.

The idea for Pick the Game came from a personal experience. My dad used to run our family football pool using Excel—filling out lines, emailing sheets, and chasing down everyone’s picks. As the pool grew, it became more time-consuming than enjoyable. That’s when I decided there had to be a better way to handle it—and Pick the Game was born.

What started as a simple website to collect picks has turned into a full-fledged platform designed to make organizing your football pool easy, enjoyable, and stress-free. We take care of the logistics, so you can focus on what really matters: the competition, the camaraderie, and, of course, the game.

Whether you’re running a pool for family, friends, or coworkers, Pick the Game helps you keep everything organized and on track—no spreadsheets required. It’s all about making football season more fun, with less hassle.

Frequently Asked Questions

Pricing

How much does it cost to join a pool?

Most pools are just $1 per player for Pick’em, Confidence, or Survivor. Want to try Squares? That’s $10 per board. Super easy, right?

When do I need to pay?

You usually pay by the start of week 3, and your pool admin will handle everything through the app. No awkward cash collection needed!

Gameplay

How does a pool actually work?

It’s simple. Everyone makes their picks right in the app, you get reminders before picks are due, and the platform keeps track of scores and standings automatically. No spreadsheets. No stress.

Can I change my picks after submitting?

It depends on what the pool admin sets. They can choose to close picks before the week starts, before Sunday games, or even before each individual game. So check the pool rules—they’ll let you know when you can make changes.

Can I join more than one pool?

Absolutely! You can be in multiple pools with the same account. Switch between them easily whenever you want.

What if I pick the wrong team?

Don’t worry—everyone has bad weeks. The app tracks everything, so even if your picks flop, your standing is updated automatically. Just have fun!

Admin & Account

How many people can I add to a pool?

As many as you want! Whether it’s a small family pool or a big office league, Pick the Game can handle it.

Can I have multiple admins?

Yep! You can promote friends or colleagues to admin so they can help manage picks and keep everything running smoothly.

How do I invite people?

Super easy! Share an invite link, a QR code, or send them an email right from the app. No printing, no fuss.

Can friends from other cities join?

Absolutely! Pick the Game works anywhere, so long-distance friends or family can join in the fun too.
