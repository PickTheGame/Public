Built because football pools should feel fun, not clerical.

PickTheGame started with the familiar routine of emailed spreadsheets, missed picks, and one organizer doing all the cleanup. The platform was built to remove that friction and keep the season centered on the people playing.

Today it gives families, friend groups, and workplaces a cleaner way to run football pools, from invites and weekly picks to standings and reminders.

Common questions, answered plainly.

Everything here is designed to help someone decide quickly whether PickTheGame fits their pool.

Pricing

How much does it cost to join a pool?

Most Pick'em, Confidence, and Survivor pools start at $1 per player. Squares are $10 per board.

When does PickTheGame charge for a pool?

PickTheGame charges the pool commissioner for the full pool starting in week 3. Individual players do not pay PickTheGame directly.

Gameplay

How does a pool work?

Players submit picks in the app, reminders go out before deadlines, and the standings update automatically as games finish.

Can players change picks after submitting?

That depends on the lock rules the organizer chooses. Pools can lock before the week starts, before Sunday games, or before each game.

Can one account join multiple pools?

Yes. Players can move between pools from the same account without starting over.

Admin

How many people can join?

Small family pools and large office groups both work. The platform is built to scale with the size of the season.

Can there be multiple admins?

Yes. Organizers can promote other people to help manage invites, settings, and pool upkeep.

How do invitations work?

Invite links, QR codes, and direct email invitations keep onboarding simple for the whole group.
