1. What Information Do We Collect?

Personal Information You Provide

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Password

Sensitive Information: We do not collect or process sensitive personal data.

Payment Data

Payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store payment card details. See Stripe’s Privacy Policy at https://stripe.com/privacy.

Application & Device Data

If you use our mobile application, we may collect device and usage information including IP address, device identifiers, operating system, app features accessed, and diagnostics.

Push Notifications

You may opt out of push notifications through your device settings.

Information Collected Automatically

We collect log data, usage data, device information, and approximate location data to maintain security and performance.

Google API Use

Our use of Google APIs complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including Limited Use requirements.
